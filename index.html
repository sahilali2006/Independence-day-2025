About Independence Day

Independence Day of India is celebrated every year on 15th August. It marks the day in 1947 when India gained freedom from British rule. This day is celebrated with flag hoisting ceremonies, parades, cultural events, and remembering the sacrifices made by our freedom fighters.

History

The struggle for India’s independence was a long and challenging journey. Leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Bhagat Singh, Subhas Chandra Bose, and many others played a crucial role in mobilizing the masses and leading various movements against British rule. After years of struggle, India finally became a free nation on 15th August 1947.
